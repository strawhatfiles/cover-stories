Canon Specials (25min & 16min)

Monsters: 103 Mercies Dragon Damnation (2024)

This is an adaptation of a one-shot, that was written by Oda in 1994, before he started working on One Piece.

Despite being a standalone, its main lead is part of One Piece canon.

Viewable on Netflix

One Piece Film Strong World: Episode 0 (2010)

This is an adaptation of "Chapter 0", which was written by Oda and released alongside one of the chapters during Marineford.

The content of this episode is canon & good to have as context going into Movie #10 (One Piece Film: Strong World).

⚠️ For some reason, however, they thought it would be a good idea to basically spoil the majority of the movie in the preview for this episode... So be careful & skip about a quarter of the way into the video, and then backtrack to 1:45
